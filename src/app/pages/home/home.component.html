Access to free one-to-one lessons

Introducing a free education app designed for students affected by natural disasters, those who have fallen behind in their studies, and girls who do not have access to education! This app provides access to qualified one-to-one lessons at no cost, allowing students to experience personalized learning and bridge the academic gap. Our expert teachers are specifically prepared to help make up for the education losses caused by disasters and to provide access to education for girls who might otherwise be excluded. Therefore, our app has been developed to support all students affected by disasters, those who are lagging behind in their studies, and girls who are not able to access education, completely free of charge.
